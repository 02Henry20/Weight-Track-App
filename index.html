Averaging and models

i

Averages nearby weigh-ins to reduce daily water and digestion noise. Use 1 day for no smoothing.

i

Controls how many recent days are used to estimate weight velocity and the current diet phase. Labels are Cut, Maintain, Bulk, Diet break and Deload. Deload is a low-confidence recovery-pattern guess because training load is not tracked.

i

Shows maintenance-model readiness, measurement sufficiency and weight volatility together beside the chart.

i

Maintenance uses average logged kcal/day minus the weight-change slope times the kcal/kg setting. Missing calorie days lower coverage, so a week with only weight can make the estimate poor. The button picks the window with the best blend of calorie coverage, weight count, confidence and uncertainty.
